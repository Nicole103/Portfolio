My story

My name is Nicole Heersink. Let me tell you a little bit about myself. I am originally from Phoenix, Arizona where the sun always burns hot, trees are a thing of mystery and any weather other than hot is a myth. About 12 years ago, I made the bold decision to move to the beautiful state of Colorado to attend college. While attending college in Denver I met my amazing husband. We have been married for 9 years and we love spending time in the great outdoors.

I am a person who enjoys a good challenge and that energy carries over into my professional life as well. After graduating college, I jumped into the work force as a project manager. Though that job is challenging and fast moving, I have been wanting to learn a new skill to improve my career path. This summer, I found the Denver University Web Development Bootcamp program and was instantly intrigued to learn this skill. I am very excited to embark on this journey and see where it takes my career!
